## Business Insights

## Product Performance:
HeadPhones Accusantium was the best-selling product, generating $0.86M in sales and $142K in profit, making it the company's highest-performing individual product.
Within the Home Décor and Furnishings category, Sofa ($15.2M) and Lamp ($14.8M) were the top revenue contributors.
Fiction Books generated the lowest sales within the product portfolio, indicating an opportunity to review pricing, promotions, or product assortment.
Lucknow emerged as the most profitable city, contributing approximately $142K in profit, making it a strategically important market for future business expansion.
## Seasonal Trends:

May was the strongest-performing month, generating over $50M in sales and more than $7M in profit, indicating a significant seasonal demand peak.
Sales reached their lowest levels during February and September, highlighting periods of weaker customer demand.
2024 was the company's best-performing year, generating over $271M in sales with an impressive 324% year-over-year growth.
Sales declined by 11.8% in 2025, suggesting the need to investigate potential factors such as changing market conditions, pricing strategies, or customer demand.

## Regional Trends:

The North Region was the highest-performing market, contributing $143.6M (26.9%) in sales and $21.34M (26.78%) in total profit.
Although the North (1,288 orders) and South (1,215 orders) recorded similar order volumes, the North generated substantially higher revenue. This suggests that customers in the North purchased higher-value products or placed larger orders.
